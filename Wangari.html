Wangari Maathai's Tribute

Wangari Maathai's timeline.


  • April 1940 - Born in the little village of Nyeri District.

  • September 1960 - Scholarship to study at Mt. Scholastica college in Atchison Kansas where she majored in biological with minors in chemistry and Germany.

  • 1946 - Received her Bachelor of science degree.

  • 1966 - Received her Msc. in biological sciences which was funded by the Africa-America Institute and was appointed as research assistant to a professor of zoology at University College of Nairobi and got to Nairobi to find that the position had been given to someone else

  • 1966 - Professor Reinhold Hoffman from University of Giessen offered her a job as a research assistant in the micro-anatomy section of the newly established department of vet anatomy in the school of vet medicine at the University College of Nairobi
